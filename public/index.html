About Us

A Sanctuary for the Soul

Prathibhimba is a boutique retreat set in the quiet, verdant surroundings of Madikeri, Coorg. Designed for travelers who seek an escape into nature without sacrificing comfort, the retreat offers a serene space to unwind and reconnect.

With thoughtfully curated rooms and warm hospitality, Prathibhimba provides an elevated experience for families, couples, and groups visiting Madikeri. Its ideal location offers both peaceful seclusion and easy access to the most captivating attractions of the region.
