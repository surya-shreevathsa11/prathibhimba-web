About Us

Wake Up by the Lake

Prathibhimba is a Coorg homestay with lake view near Madikeri, set in the peaceful outskirts surrounded by coffee estates. Wake up to still waters, fresh air, and quiet surroundings designed for those who want a break from crowded, commercial stays.

With two private rooms and comfortable dorms, it’s ideal for couples, families, and groups looking for a relaxed and memorable stay in Madikeri. Enjoy the balance of privacy, scenic beauty, and easy access to Coorg’s top attractions, all while experiencing genuine, attentive hospitality.

How To Find Us

Directions

How to Reach Prathibhimba

Prathibhimba is located in Maragodu, a peaceful village on the outskirts of Madikeri, surrounded by coffee estates and natural greenery.

The stay is well connected by road. From Bangalore, it’s approximately a 5–6 hour drive (around 260 km). From Madikeri town, Prathibhimba is about a 20–30 minute drive through scenic estate roads.

Once you reach Madikeri, head towards Maragodu village. Detailed directions and the exact location will be shared after booking for a smooth arrival.
